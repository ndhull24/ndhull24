# 💫 About Me:
I am a data and AI professional who works at the intersection of analytics, machine learning, and business strategy. My work focuses on turning messy, real-world data into structured insights, predictive models, and scalable data systems that drive decision-making.<br><br>I have experience designing data pipelines, analytics platforms, and forecasting models using tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, and modern ML frameworks. I enjoy building systems that move from raw signals—like documents, call transcripts, or market data—to actionable intelligence through ETL pipelines, modeling, and visualization.<br><br>Professionally, I have worked in roles spanning data analytics, research analysis, and AI engineering, contributing to projects in areas such as competitive intelligence, CRM/ERP analytics, demand forecasting, and conversational AI systems. My approach combines structured thinking, statistical analysis, and practical engineering, allowing me to bridge technical work with business outcomes.<br><br>I am also deeply interested in AI systems and emerging technologies, frequently experimenting with LLMs, speech analytics, voice cloning, and video generation pipelines. I like understanding how these technologies work under the hood and building practical implementations.<br><br>At my core, I’m someone who enjoys solving complex problems with data, communicating insights clearly, and continuously learning new technologies to stay ahead in the evolving data and AI landscape.
